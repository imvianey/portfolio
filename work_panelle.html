Building Panelle

Information Architecture

  • Parents and children sign in separately
  • Send each other links with activation codes to link accounts
  • The parents main purpose is to monitor their child’s activity
  • Children are operating the bulk of the content, need straightforward navigation

Paper Prototype

The first iteration of the paper prototype was to visualize the placement of various features.

The second iteration was to test the logistics behind logging in for both the child and the parent. I also wanted to explore the differences between the parent portal and the child’s account.

  • Largest hurdle: Figuring out how to connect the child and parent accounts
  • Importance of Wording: in wording the instructions is necessary so users aren’t confused
  • Easy access to all children’s accounts

Usability Testing

Scenario

  • Browse and Buy
  • Browse and Preview

Approach

  • Moderated Usability Testing
  • Design Prototype Testing

Results

  • The users thought it was very straight forward and organized
  • Liked that it was focused for their age group
  • Liked the wishlist feature to show parents what they want to buy
