SPOT

Photography Location Scouting App

The Problem

Location scouting is difficult and time-consuming. How might we help student photographers find photography locations? How might we simplify the photography process for student photographers?

The Solution

Providing and app with resources designed to assist in discovering new and interesting photography locations without having to go through rigorous pre-planning phases.

Target User

Constant access to photography with phone camera.

Interested in exploring and photographing locations in the local area.

Mostly contained to his local area and has trouble finding new photography locations.

Dan posts on Instagram and gets feedback through the comments.

Solving The Problem

Experience Map

The experience map recognized the users need for a bookmark button so the user could save a location or image. It is valuable in providing solutions for possible problems and new features.

Scenario

Dan needs to get his photography assignment before the deadline but cannot find the right backgrop for his model.

Goals

Find a photography location easily without extensive research.

Storyboard

1

Dan wants to take a picture but doesn't know where.

2

3

4

5
